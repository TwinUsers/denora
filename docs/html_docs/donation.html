Denora is an open source, free software. However the developers have put a lot of time, money and effort into making the program what it is today.

If you want to assist us or show your appreciation to the development team, use the SourceForge linke below. You are not required to send anything and you can feel free to ignore this. We're not asking for anything huge, whatever you can afford is fine.

Donation Link: http://sourceforge.net/donate/index.php?group_id=137267

If you don't want to use SourceForge to donate, contact as at dev@denorastats.org before sending anything.

All donators will be listed on the website (regardless of the amount). To make this possible, please include along with your donation the name (or nickname) you want to appear in the list as well as the company name (or IRC server) to list.

Thank you for your support,
The Denora Team
